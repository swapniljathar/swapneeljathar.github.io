Who I Am

I am a 14-year-old innovator, social entrepreneur, speaker and founder of ReThink. I am currently attending Neuqua Valley High School as a freshman in Naperville, Illinois. My groundbreaking research has led me to several innovative solutions to problems facing our society today, including areas such as cyberbullying.

I have had the opportunity to speak at various national and international platforms, schools and communities to raise awareness about the scourge of cyberbullying, stop the hate on internet and engage adoloscents to spread positivity and tolerance around the world.

What I Do

I have always been fascinated with the inner workings of the brain and how it impacts our lives. In 2012, after the death of my aunt in a road-side accident, I immersed myself into researching cognitive distraction of the brain and finding effective solutions to prevent fatal accidents due to distracted driving. Later, in 2013, I was shocked to hear about a young girl’s suicide due to bullying on the Internet and set out to find a long-term solution to cyberbullying. I created a scientifically proven, award winning and patented software product called ReThink, which stops cyberbullying at the source before the damage is done. I am passionate about this issue and look forward to the day when we have conquered cyberbullying.

My Work

Passionate to stop cyberbullying in adolescents, I created the patented product "ReThink" that stops cyberbullying at the source, before the bullying occurs, before the damage is done! My research has found that with "Rethink", adolescents change their mind 93% of the time and decide not to post an offensive message. I was selected as Google Global Science Fair Finalists 2014 for my work on "ReThink". I have availed myself of every opportunity I have had to spread awareness about the scourge of cyberbullying and how Rethink can help. I have spoken about this issue at various national and international platforms, conferences, forums and schools.

I know we will need all the help we can get to help stop cyberbullying. Please contact me to find out how we can work together to raise awareness about the issue of cyberbullying and stop this pandemic from hurting our schools and communities.

Also, be part of the change we want in this world, join the ReThink Movement!
