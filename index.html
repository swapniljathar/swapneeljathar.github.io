Who I Am

What I Do

I have always been fascinated with the inner workings of the brain and how it impacts our lives. I am both versatile and specialized, modern and old-fashioned, rebellious and obedient, all at one go. I love to read, write, ask, talk, listen, solve problems, and cherish ideas. I like to work round the clock, go partying on weekends, and compete with myself incessantly but I also like to spend a quiet day reading a novel on the banks of a pond.

I love my country, culture, and heritage, and I also love rebelling against the prevailing stereotypes in my society. I love being ambitious, but I love being simple even more. I can be an extrovert and the soul of a party, and I can also keep a low profile and move quietly, both with a healthy confidence and priority to living the moment. If there could be an example of opposites turning into complements, that would be me.

My future:

I see myself as all of the above – and more.

On the career front, I aim to start with research and reach a position of influence, taking decisions that would make a positive difference in the lives of ordinary people; status, authority and financial gain are byproducts.

Entrepreneurship? Still toying with the idea. Yet, wherever I work and whatever I do, I will be contributing all this, and much more…
